Security is at the core of everything we do at Factlink. Our team follows industry-leading best practices to protect user funds and data.

## Key Security Measures

- **Smart Contract Audits:** All contracts are regularly audited by top security firms.
- **Bug Bounty Programs:** We incentivize the community to find and report vulnerabilities.
- **Multi-Sig Wallets:** Critical operations require multiple approvals.
- **Continuous Monitoring:** Automated systems watch for suspicious activity 24/7.

## User Safety Tips

- Always verify URLs and contract addresses.
- Use hardware wallets for large holdings.
- Enable two-factor authentication where possible.

## Our Commitment

Factlink is dedicated to transparency and rapid response. If you discover a vulnerability, please contact our security team immediately.

Together, we can keep DeFi safe and secure for everyone.
