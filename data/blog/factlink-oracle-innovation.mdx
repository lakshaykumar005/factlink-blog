Oracles are a critical component of any blockchain ecosystem. Factlink's decentralized oracle network ensures that smart contracts receive accurate and tamper-proof data from the real world.

## How Factlink Oracles Work

Factlink oracles aggregate data from multiple sources, validate it, and deliver it to smart contracts. This process eliminates single points of failure and increases trust in decentralized applications.

## Use Cases

- **DeFi Markets:** Price feeds for synthetic assets and derivatives.
- **Prediction Markets:** Reliable event outcomes for market resolution.
- **Insurance:** Automated claims based on real-world data.

## Why Choose Factlink Oracles?

- **Decentralization:** No single entity controls the data.
- **Security:** Robust validation and consensus mechanisms.
- **Scalability:** Designed to support a wide range of blockchain applications.

Factlink oracles are paving the way for a more secure and reliable blockchain future.
