Prediction markets are powerful tools for aggregating information and forecasting outcomes. Factlink brings these markets to the blockchain, making them more accessible, transparent, and secure.

## What Are Prediction Markets?

Prediction markets allow users to buy and sell shares in the outcome of future events. The market price reflects the collective wisdom of participants.

## Factlink's Role

Factlink provides the infrastructure for decentralized prediction markets, ensuring:

- **Security:** Smart contracts and oracles guarantee fair outcomes.
- **Transparency:** All trades and outcomes are recorded on-chain.
- **Accessibility:** Anyone can participate, anywhere in the world.

## Real-World Impact

From elections to sports to financial forecasts, Factlink-powered prediction markets are shaping the future of information trading.
