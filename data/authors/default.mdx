The Factlink Protocol Team is dedicated to building decentralized financial infrastructure and products on the Ethereum blockchain. Factlink enables the creation of synthetic assets and decentralized oracles, empowering global access to financial markets.

Our mission is to make financial markets universally accessible and trustless. We collaborate with projects like Polymarket to expand the reach and utility of blockchain technology.
